\solution
The parametric equation for AB is given by
\begin{align}
\vec{x} &= \vec{A} + k\vec{m}\\
\text{where, } \vec{m} &= \vec{B} -\vec{A}\\
&= \myvec{3 \\ -5} -\myvec{-3\\ -5}\\
&= \myvec{6 \\0}
\end{align}
Hence we get,
\begin{align}
AB: \vec{x} = &\myvec{-3\\-5} + k \myvec{6\\0}
\end{align}
Similarly, 
\begin{align}
BC: \vec{x} = &\myvec{3\\-5} + k \myvec{-7\\2}\\
CA: \vec{x} = &\myvec{-4\\-3} + k \myvec{1\\-2}
\end{align}
